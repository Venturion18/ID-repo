Full Name: Chrissy Nicola Costanza

Birthday: August 23, 1995

Age: 25 years (2020)

Born in: New Jersey, United States

Position: Lead vocalist
Chrissy Costanza was introduced to Dan Gow and Will Ferri through a mutual friend in 2011, joining their band. She wrote most of the lyrics for their songs, and is the main vocalist for the group.
Full Name: Daniel Gow

Birthday: December 8, 1995

Age: 25 years (2020)

Daniel Gow was also joined the group in 2011, taking up the role of guitarist and backing vocalist.
Full Name: William Ferri

Birthday: April 25, 1996

Age: 24 years (2020)

Born in: Lagrangeville, New York

Position: Bassist
Will was friends with Dan Gow and they met Chrissy Costanza through a mutual friend, eventually forming the group in 2011. He takes up the role of bassist.
